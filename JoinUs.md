# **Join us**

BigBatDatabase is a community-driven initiative to compile, standardize, and share bat trait data for research, education, and conservation. We welcome everyone, especially researchers, students, collection managers, field biologists, and other contributors interested in bat biodiversity and data synthesis.

**If you have any questions or would like to join our Slack community, please contact us at [bigbatdatabase@gmail.com](mailto:bigbatdatabase@gmail.com).**

To help us process your request, please briefly introduce yourself and provide a link to a publication, professional profile, or other online resource that we can use to verify your identity.

We look forward to connecting with you and building BigBatDatabase together.
